> Calculate Stats of the mutant the value of the variable use the Stats.csv file it's separated with "|":
Example:
Specimen|Name|Speed|odds|dna|Life|incubMin|atk1|atk1p|atk2|atk2p|bank|unlockattack|Type|reciper|abilities|abilityPct1|abilityPct2|orbSlots
Specimen_A_01| Robot|250|40| A|1819|5| 274:AOE| 413:AOE| 549| 825|42| 1:1:a;2:5:neutre;1p:10:a;2p:15:neutre| | | 1:ability_shield;2:ability_shield_plus|20|30| n;s:20h

Global Values:
Adjust= 100
Value of the Stars:
Platinum=100
Gold=75
Silver=30
Bronze=10
Basic=0
BonusReactor=Necesita Archivo gacha.csv
BonusStar= 100 + Valor de la Estrella Seleccionada(Stars)


1. Calculate the level of the Mutant (Min. value= 25):
Level= 100+10*(FameLevelInsertedbyUser - 1)
2. Calculate Life of the Mutant:
LifeF=(Life*BonusStar*Level*Adjust)/1000000
3. Calculate Attack1 and Attack2:
Atk1F=(atk1p*BonusStar*Level*Adjust)/1000000
Atk2F=(atk2p*BonusStar*Level*Adjust)/1000000
4. Calcuate the ability of both Attacks (splitted)
(Atk1)AbilityF= Atk1F*abilityPct2
(Atk2)AbilityF2= Atk2F*abilityPct2
5. Calculate the speed:
SpeedF=10/(Speed/100)

6. Final Values to show:
Specimen and Name
Type
Level
LifeF
SpeedF
Atk1F and (Atk1)AbilityF
Atk2F and (Atk2)AbilityF
Name of the ability (use column "abilities" i.e.: 1:ability_shield, just keep "Shield")
